YoYoga

YoYoga is a health oriented app that is helps people not only to keep fit, but also to improve health condition.

The Project

YoYoga will provide yoga sequences based on the user's health problems (asthma, headaches, stress, back pain, blood pressure etc). App will have an "asana dictionary" where every asana will be described with recommendations and cautions. Besides health oriented sequences, here will be different practices: for different body parts ( arms, abs, legs), for stretching, restoration, etc. The application will allow user to train with a friend remotely by creating a "classroom" where the users can set the class that will begin at the certain time by running the chosen sequence. This function will help people to encourage and inspire each other not to skip practices

The Problem

The people are not being able to find the proper infrastructure to ride their bikes around the city and are looking for a digital service solution

Users

Users interviewed

  • People who think about using a bike as their main mode for transportation, but are not fully convinced yet
  • Biking beginners who need assistance and want to get to know the city better
  • Biking experts who are mainly looking for a powerful navigation tool

  • What do you like in the app you use and what don't you like?
  • Could you tell me about the time you had any difficulty while cycling, or even an accident (if it ever happened)?

    Solution

    For the card-sorting I prepared 5 categories which were supposed to contain certain components (based on the planned structure of my application):
    • Profile – user’s profile (also a homepage)
    • Asanas – collection of single yoga poses with pictures and descriptions
    • Sequences – yoga practices
    • Special needs sequences – practices oriented on special health issues
    • Joint sessions – ability to practice with another user
    The users’ task was to intuitively sort the offered cards to see how well they would understand the app’s structure.
