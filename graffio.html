Graffio

Graffio is a mobile application that will help users to become
aware of the Carbon Footprint they leave after each trip.

The Project

Graffio was my Master's project in the Technological Unniversity Dublin. It is a mobile app that brings users awareness of the carbon footprint they leave with every journey. The app was built in ReactJS and deployed for Android platform

The Problem

Many people are not aware of the environmental impact they make while making a single journey.

My Role

UI/UX Designer, App developer

Approach

Users

Users interviewed

  • Young people 20-30 who travel more than 5 times a year

    • Questions asked

  • How often do you travel? Per year?
  • How do you usually travel?
  • Have you ever thought of the carbon footprint you leave every time you travel?
  • What would motivate you to travel more sustainably?
  • If you knew your carbon footprint, will it make you to reconsider your ways of travel?
  • Do you cycle?
  • What do you think of travellinng by bicycle?
